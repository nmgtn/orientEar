Orientear

Orientear will be a browser-based ear trainer, taking advantage of HTML5 and the Web Audio API, building upon the work done on BeaqleJS. It is being developed by Andy Normington as a Technical Project (final technical assessment) for the Tonmeister course at the Institute of Sound Recording, part of the University of Surrey.
